## What's Inside

VCs make their Series A decision in the 12 months after your seed — most founders don't realize the clock is running. This checklist covers every metric, signal, and readiness indicator that determines whether you get the meeting.

You'll get:

- **Revenue metrics checklist** — MRR growth rate, ARR targets, pipeline coverage, and what "consistent" actually means to a Series A investor
- **Retention benchmarks** — Net Revenue Retention, Gross Revenue Retention, churn thresholds, and what below-100% NRR signals to VCs
- **Team & execution checklist** — hiring velocity, product shipping speed, and the decision-making cadence that separates fundable teams from ones that stall
- **Investor-readiness checklist** — data room, Series A narrative, target investor list, and when to actually start outreach

## Who This Is For

This checklist is for seed-funded founders in the 12–18 months after closing their seed round. If you're building toward a Series A and aren't sure whether your metrics are where they need to be — this checklist will tell you exactly what's missing and what to fix first.

It's also for founders who've had Series A meetings that didn't convert. In most cases, the answer is in the retention or revenue consistency numbers. This checklist helps you find it.
