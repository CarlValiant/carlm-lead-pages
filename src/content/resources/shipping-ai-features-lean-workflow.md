## What's Inside

This guide lays out the exact 5-step workflow for shipping a production AI feature without hiring a team or raising a round. You'll learn how to separate the **thinking** (Claude or ChatGPT), the **building** (Manus), and the **owning** (GitHub + your own hosting) into a repeatable process that compresses months of engineering work into a weekend.

You'll get:

- **The 5-step workflow** — architect prompts, hand off to an AI agent, test, wire into your product via REST/WebSockets/gRPC, and self-host on infrastructure you own
- **A realistic starter prompt** you can copy, customize, and send to Claude or ChatGPT to generate a build-ready spec for any AI feature
- **What this replaces and what it doesn't** — clear guidance on where this workflow shines (MVPs, prototypes, single-purpose features) and where you still need senior engineering judgment
- **6 honest limitations** every founder should plan around before shipping AI-generated code to production

## Who This Is For

This guide is for solo founders, indie developers, and small studio teams who have an AI feature on their roadmap but assumed they needed to hire ML engineers or raise capital to build it. If you've been sitting on an AI idea because the cost and timeline felt out of reach, this workflow shows you how to ship it yourself — and own every line of code when you're done.
